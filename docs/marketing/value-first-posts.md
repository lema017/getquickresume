Many qualified candidates get rejected before a recruiter even reads their resume — and most people don’t know why.

The reason is something called an **ATS (Applicant Tracking System).**

Companies use these systems to automatically filter resumes based on keywords, formatting, and relevance. If your resume is not optimized for ATS, it may never reach human eyes — even if you are highly qualified.
