# Premium Checkout Page Image - Generation Prompt

## Prompt for AI Image Generator

```
Elegant isometric 3D illustration of a golden document with a checkmark badge floating above a minimalist desk setup. Soft gradient background transitioning from deep navy to warm gold. In the top-center, display a rounded square logo with blue-to-purple gradient containing a white letter "G", with "GetQuickResume" text beside it in clean white typography. Premium feel with subtle glowing accents. Clean corporate style. Professional and trustworthy mood. Modern flat design with gentle shadows. Square format 1:1 aspect ratio, 800x800 pixels.
```

## Image Specifications

- **Aspect Ratio**: 1:1 (square)
- **Dimensions**: 800 × 800 pixels
- **Style**: Professional, Corporate, Clean
- **Colors**: Navy blue (#1e3a8a), Gold/Amber (#f59e0b), White
- **Logo Details**: 
  - Rounded square with gradient (#3B82F6 → #8B5CF6)
  - White letter "G" in center
  - "GetQuickResume" text in bold, dark gray or white

## Important Note

AI image generators often struggle with accurate text and logos. For best results:

1. **Generate the base image** using the prompt above (without logo/text)
2. **Add logo and text manually** using:
   - Figma
   - Canva
   - Photoshop
   - GIMP (free)

This ensures your branding looks exactly as intended.
